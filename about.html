About Us

OBNESS FLOWERS was founded in 2020 by Ruth Petros. We are located at 22 Bole Road, Tesheab Building, Addis Ababa.

Ruth Petros

Ruth is a young and passionate woman — a housewife, visionary entrepreneur, and a Christian believer. She is not only the heart behind OBNESS FLOWERS but also a TikToker and a voice for women. Ruth uses her platform to inspire and support women facing challenges, especially those involving gender issues. She believes in building a business that spreads joy through flowers while also promoting hope, empowerment, and faith.
